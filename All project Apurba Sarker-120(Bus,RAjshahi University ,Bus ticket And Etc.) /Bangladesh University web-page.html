About the Administration Building

The Bangladesh University Administration Building serves as the central hub for all administrative functions of the university. Constructed in 1963, this iconic structure has been the nerve center of the university's operations for over six decades.

Housing the offices of the Vice-Chancellor, Registrar, Treasurer, and other key administrative departments, this building is where important decisions regarding the university's governance, academic policies, and development plans are made.

With Its distinctive architectural style blending modern and traditional elements, the Administration Building stands as a symbol of the university's commitment to excellence in higher education and its role as a leading institution in Bangladesh.

Key Administrative Departments

Office of the Vice-Chancellor

The highest executive position in the university, responsible for overall administration and academic leadership.

Registrar's Office

Manages student admissions, academic records, examinations, and faculty appointments.

Treasurer's Office

Oversees the university's finances, budgeting, and financial planning.
